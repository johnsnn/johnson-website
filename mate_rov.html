MATE ROV Tools

About the Project

During my time with the Fish Logic Underwater Robotics team, I designed innovative tools for our Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) that significantly enhanced task efficiency, contributing to our high school team's first runner-up finish in the EXPLORER CLASS at the 2023 MATE World Championships, despite competing against university teams. This experience honed my engineering and problem-solving skills and allowed me to build strong social connections while learning about unique designs from university teams worldwide.

Awards

- First Runner-Up: 2023 Marine Advanced Technology Education (MATE) ROV Competition World Championships (United States)

- Third Place: 2023 Marine Advanced Technology Education (MATE) ROV Competition Regionals (Hong Kong)
