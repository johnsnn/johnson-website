Robotic Arm

About the Project

Developed during middle school as my initial exploration into STEM, this robotic arm project is powered by a micro:bit and driven by micro servos, enabling precise three-axis movement. All components were custom-designed and 3D modeled using Autodesk Inventor, with control implemented through the MakeCode programming software.
